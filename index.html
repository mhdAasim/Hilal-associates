Frequently Asked Questions

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a value-added tax levied on most goods and services sold for domestic consumption. It helps streamline taxation and makes compliance easier for businesses.

You can register for GST through the government portal, but our team can handle the entire process for you to make it quick and hassle-free!

Late GST filing can result in penalties and interest charges. We help you stay compliant and file your returns on time to avoid unnecessary fees.

You’ll need documents like PAN card, proof of business registration, identity and address proof, and bank account details. We can guide you through the entire documentation process!

You can claim ITC on purchases used for business purposes, but certain restrictions apply. Our team can help you maximize your ITC claims while staying compliant with GST laws.
